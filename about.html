My definition of game

There are 4 main ones, achievers, killers, explorers, and socializers. The achievers like to play to win things and gain the most. The killers like to play to impose their will on other people. They like to watch the world burn. The explorers like to discover new things and explore the worlds. The socializers play to have fun with other people. My two main types are achiever and killer.
